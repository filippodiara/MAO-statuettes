Information about the 3D Collection

 

This collection is referred to the 3D documentation project of Terracotta Statuettes from Seleucia on Tigris (IRAQ) preserved inside the Oriental Art Museum (MAO) carried out by University of Turin on behalf of the ITSERR - Resilience European project. The 3D documentation was performed by using non invasive structured-light scanners. Historical metadata are linked to 3D models.

 

How to open and explore 3D models of cretulae:

1) Select the desired model depending on museum ID;

2) Put the password you have recived in order to open the private 3D visualization;

3) Click on 3D play logo

4) Use the mouse to navigate the model: left click + mouvement to rotate the model; mouse wheel to zoom it; right click + mouvement to shift the model;

5) If you prefer, you can visualize the 3D model in full screen mode by clicking the dedicated button in the bottom right-hand corner;

6) You can visualize the model also without the texture layer by clicking on Model Inspector and then Matcap. To reset the view click the Final Render mode.
